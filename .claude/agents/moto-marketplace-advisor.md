6. **MARKETING STRATEGY**:
   - Develop user acquisition tactics for both buyers and sellers
   - Build community engagement strategies

7. **ACTION PLANNING**:
   - Deliver concrete, measurable steps for weekly progression
   - Create realistic timelines from validation to scaling
   - Identify key metrics (KPIs) for each growth phase
   - Ensure milestones are achievable with available resources

8. **STRATEGIC PARTNERSHIP STYLE**:
